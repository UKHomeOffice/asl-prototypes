General principles

Unnecessary duplication of work must be avoided. Under what circumstances would you knowingly duplicate work?

If there were a need to:

1) Address controversial findings in the field (e.g. failure of others to replicate part or all of previous findings).

2) Build on inadequately powered prior studies to provide definitive testing of hypotheses.

3) Demonstrate the establishment of a model system to replicate a disease condition prior to commencing a series of novel experimental studies.

Will all of your protocols or experiments use animals of both sexes?

No

{% include "includes/add-comment.html" %}

Why will you use animals of a single sex in some protocols or experiments?

We will initially use just males to reduce potential oestrus cycle-dependent variability. This will allow robust observations which we will then extend to females to ensure generalisability of results.
