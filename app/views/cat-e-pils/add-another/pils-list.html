Personal licence reviews

Personal licences must be reviewed every 5 years to confirm they are still in use and up to date. The licence holder or an admin can complete a review.

If a licence requires no changes, it can be reviewed by an NTCO on behalf of the licence holder up to 12 weeks before the deadline.
