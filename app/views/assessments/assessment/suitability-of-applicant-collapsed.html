Position within the establishment

  • Applicants must be in an appropriate position of authority to take responsibility for the conduct of the programme of work
  • There must be no real or perceived conflicts of interest

Education

Applicants have:

  • Expert knowledge in the scientific discipline relating to the area of work
  • Specific knowledge of the biology, physiology and husbandry requirements of the species that will be used in the programme of work
  • A good knowledge of the methods, animal models and specialised equipment that they need to use, or close collaboration with others that do have such knowledge
  • Appropriate education and training in designing programmes of work under ASPA
  • Expertise in experimental design (including expertise in statistics Where appropriate) or access to such expertise
  • Expertise in application of the 3Rs in the area of work
