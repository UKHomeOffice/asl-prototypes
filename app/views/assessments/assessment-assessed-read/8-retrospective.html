Retrospective assessment

A RA is required for any project in the following categories:

  • All projects using non-human primates (NHPs)
  • All projects involving procedures prospectively classified as severe
  • All projects using cats, dogs or equidae
  • All projects using endangered animals
  • Teaching licences
  • Any project raising important animal welfare or ethical concerns, novel or contentious issues or societal concerns

The RA date will be on expiry or revocation of the project licence unless there is a specific reason for undertaking retrospective assessment during the course of the project, or to undertake more than one RA
