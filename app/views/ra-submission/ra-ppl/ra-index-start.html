Creating your retrospective assessment

Any information you enter in your retrospective assessment will form part of a non-technical summary (NTS) on GOV.UK, so use everyday language that can be easily understood by members of the public.

As a retrospective assessment is a public document, do not include any information that could identify any people, locations, or intellectual property related to your project. You will be able to review and edit your NTS as a whole before you send your licence application to the Home Office.
