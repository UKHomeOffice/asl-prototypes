Objectives

Key Aims/Objectives of this project are:

1. To develop models of human blood diseases (Acute myeloid leukaemia, acute lymphoblastic leukaemia, myelodysplastic syndrome, Diamond-Blackfan Anaemia) using the zebrafish or kill fish (haematopoietic disease of aging).

2. To use these models to delineate disease mechanisms for these conditions by studying the effects of gene deletions or overexpression on the developmental haematopoiesis or the growing juvenile and adult zebrafish or kill fish.

3. To use these models to identify novel therapeutics for these conditions by performing small molecule screens on zebrafish embryos and adults or kill fish.
