Establishments

To change the primary establishment you must:

  • review any incomplete sections of the project licence
  • have the PEL holders' endorsement from both establishments. These will be requested
    once you submit the application
  • take the application through the AWERB review process at the proposed primary establishment and any additional establishments.

! Warning Once it has been endorsed by a PEL holder, you will no longer be able to change the primary establishment as part of this amendment.

Why is the establishment I need not listed?

If the establishment is not listed, ask the Home Office Liaison Contact (HOLC) of that establishment to send you an invitation. Once you accept the invitation, you can request your licence to be transferred.
