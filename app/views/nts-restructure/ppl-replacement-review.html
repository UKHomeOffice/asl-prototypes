Replacement

{% include "includes/nts-warning-small.html" %}

We have set up and tested a method to investigate atherosclerosis resulting from radiation in cultured cells isolated from humans, including young and old donors to determine age effects in this non-animal alternative model. Having established the mechanism of the early steps involved in radiation-induced plaque formation and assessed the effect of drugs in this system, it is necessary to confirm these results in an animal model.

Edit

Numerous resources including NC3Rs, PREPARE guidelines and Norecopa have been used to investigate additional non-animal alternatives.

Edit

Lower species such as non-vertebrates and fish are not appropriate for this research as they have very different circulatory systems and have not been proven to be a good model for studying atherosclerosis.
